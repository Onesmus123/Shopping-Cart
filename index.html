Who We Are

Founded in August 2023 and based in Katulani Sub-County, Kitui County, we specialize in raising hardy Galla Goats and Dorper Sheep through a free-range system that respects nature and delivers real results.


From a humble start with just a few animals, Rasta FARM has grown into a space of learning, inspiration, and steady progress. We believe in doing things the natural way - with consistency, care, and pure good vibes.
